Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: September 21, 2025

What Information We Collect

When you create an account, upload documents, or communicate with us, we collect the information you provide. This includes personal details like your display name and email address, as well as the content of the documents you upload for analysis. We do not store your documents after the analysis is complete. We also gather anonymous usage data to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is essential for providing our core services, such as generating document summaries and answering your legal questions. Your data helps us improve our AI models and platform functionality. We may also use your contact information to send you important service updates and support messages.

How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your data very seriously. All information is encrypted both in transit (using TLS) and at rest. Access is strictly limited to the automated systems required to provide our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal information. From your account, you can request to access, correct, or permanently delete your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@legalese.com.
